Description

In Civilization VI the difficulty scales up by granting the AI flat bonuses. This is lame. Instead, AI difficulty should scale more similar to how human players improve. The main 3 ways a human player can get better at strategic games like Civ VI are 1) knowing more about core game mechanics (i.e. not forgetting anything), 2) consistency in play (i.e. not making simple mistake), and 3) by increasing the planning horizon (i.e. planning 100 turns ahead instead of 10 turns ahead). Civ is also more complex because even if the AI was able to plan out an entire game length, there are multiple paths to victory, so there needs to be an overriding goal that drives the planning process.
