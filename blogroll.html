These are the best personal blogs I have found. Most of them are related to technology, but not all. I’ve also included links to some of my favorite articles. This list is pretty close to what my RSS feed reader looks like.

Author Site(s) Summary Favorites
Alex Miller alex.miller.garden Software developer & artist. Grid world
Amit Patel amitp and Red Blob Games Game developer with detailed articles on algorithms that are commonly used in game development.
Austin Henley austinhenley Software engineer & academic. Challenging projects every programmer should try and More challenging projects every programmer should try

Bret Devereaux acoup A military history professor who examines modern media with a lens of historical realism. The siege of Gondor
Cal Peterson calpaterson Software engineer specializing in Python.

Peter Norvig norvig Director of research at Google with a long and storied career in industry and academia. PyTudes (GitHub)
